Candle Wick Trimmer

A wick trimmer is an implement similar to scissors but with a small plate built-in at the tip, used to trim down the wicks of candles.

Candle Snuffer Extinguisher

A candle snuffer, candle extinguisher, or douter is an instrument used to extinguish burning candles, consisting of a small cone at the end of a handle.

Wick Dipper

A wick dipper is an elegant metal tool with a long stem with an angled end that allows you to dip the wick into the melt pool to extinguish the flame.
